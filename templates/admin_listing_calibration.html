📥 Listing Calibration

Upload listing CSVs (CarWale exports) to populate market data. Each row becomes a research_log entry tagged as Listing Aggregator, flowing into the engine's calibration data alongside verified deals.

How it works: Pick the state & city the listings were scraped from, then upload a CarWale CSV exported via Instant Data Scraper. The parser auto-resolves variants via fuzzy match, drops malformed rows with structured reasons, and inserts the rest into research_log. Listings show up as data quality medium and contribute to engine calibration but not to state-multiplier suggestions (since they are asks, not negotiated deals).
