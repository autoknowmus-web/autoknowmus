How these benchmarks are calculated

Data sources: Anonymized inventory data from 47 active Bangalore dealers contributing to AutoKnowMus. No individual dealer's stock or pricing is ever visible — only aggregate medians.

Rank meaning: "Rank 9 of 47" means ABC Motors performs better on this dimension than 38 of the 47 other Bangalore dealers. Lower rank number = better.

Refresh cadence: Benchmarks update weekly. Your rank may shift week-to-week as other dealers' data flows in.

Important honesty layer: With 47 dealers today, the statistical confidence is good but not perfect. We'll display tighter rank bands once the Bangalore dealer pool crosses 100. For very niche segments (premium European cars, vintage), dealer comparison will say "insufficient data" until coverage improves.

How market balance, scenarios, and recommendations are calculated

Market Balance: Compares Bangalore-specific buyer searches (last 30 days, from AutoKnowMus consumer side) vs active listings (from listing aggregators). Demand-heavy means ratio > 1.3×; balanced is 0.8-1.3×; supply-heavy is < 0.8×.

Three-Scenario Forecast: Each path models predicted Days to Sell (DTS), price drop or wholesale gap, holding cost (using dealer's configured ₹400/day default), and capital tie-up cost.

Capital tie-up cost: Models the opportunity cost of money locked in unsold inventory, at 1% per month of the asking value (~12% annual). Reflects the typical return a dealer could earn redeploying that capital into faster-turning stock. Adjustable in Cost Settings.

Recommendation criterion: The "★ RECOMMENDED" badge marks the path with the lowest Total Cost = price drop + holding cost + capital tie-up. This is a pure rupee comparison — no qualitative judgment.

Wholesale offer estimates: Based on observed inter-dealer transactions and typical 8-10% wholesale discount from market median. Actual offers from individual wholesale buyers will vary.

Honesty layer: Predictions use observed market patterns but aren't guarantees. Local relationships, sales pipeline, and specific car condition should override these suggestions when relevant. Wholesale exits especially depend on dealer's existing buyer network.

Common questions
Can I switch tiers later?
Yes, anytime. Upgrades apply instantly (prorated to your current billing cycle). Downgrades take effect at the end of the current cycle — you pay the higher rate for the month you're in, then start the new tier next month.
What happens after my 2-month Edge trial?
You choose. Stay on Edge, step down to Operate or Insight at the Founding Dealer rate, or fall back to Spot (free forever). Your data and inventory uploads stay with you regardless. No auto-charge — we'll always ask before billing you.
Is my data private? Will other dealers see my inventory?
Your inventory is never visible to other dealers. Dealer benchmarks and market median data are calculated from anonymized aggregates only — there's no way for another dealer to identify your specific cars or pricing. Your business is yours.
How long does setup take?
About 20 minutes. Sign in with Google, upload your current inventory (CSV or one-by-one), set your refurb / margin / holding-cost defaults, and you're done. You can be running valuations within an hour.
I'm a one-person shop — is this overkill?
Not at all — Spot tier is built for you. Upload your stock, see fair market prices, get a monthly market summary, all free forever. When your inventory grows past a handful of cars, Insight at ₹4K/month is the natural step.
Will pricing change at launch?
Regular pricing may evolve over time — but if you sign up as a Founding Dealer (first 20 paying dealers), your 20% discount is locked for the life of your account, regardless of any future price changes.
Do you sell my data?
No. We don't sell dealer inventory data to OEMs, finance companies, or third parties. The aggregated data powers AutoKnowMus' market intelligence — that's it. Your inventory makes the platform smarter for you and other dealers, not for anyone else.
