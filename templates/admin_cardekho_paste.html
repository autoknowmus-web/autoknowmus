How to use:
  1. Go to CarDekho's "Cars with prices in India" page and confirm city is set to Bangalore (top-right city selector)
  2. Optionally filter by Brand / Budget / Vehicle Type to narrow the list
  3. Expand every model accordion you want to ingest — click each model's "N Variants Matching Your Search Criteria" line to expand
  4. Press Ctrl+A, then Ctrl+C, then paste into the textarea below
  5. Click Extract preview to see all parsed variants with match status

⚠ Collapsed sections are skipped. The parser only sees variant rows that were expanded before you copied. Each unexpanded model will appear in the warnings list with its expected count.
